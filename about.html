General History

Pedigree Productions started as Pedigree Associates well over 40 years ago.

Established by Bill and Sissy Woolums and dedicated to producing the most accurate Thoroughbred Horse Pedigrees in the world. In an era without personal computers or mainframes they had a foreign racing library that was second to none, currently housed at the Virginia Horse Center.

We have produced pedigrees for many major, and minor, farms in the U.S. as well as abroad. Our clients are a Who's Who in the Thoroughbred World. We've served many auction houses in the states and have a very long standing relationship with Fasig-Tipton!

Currently Pedigree Productions is run by Sissy's daughter Anna Carney, her older children are starting to work on filling some void. Elizabeth and James Carney are now working for this great company.
