Who We Are

Pedigree Products has been in business since 1970. Founded by Sissy Woolums, she had a vision of delivering superior quality pedigree’s to our clients with superior customer service. This business model has treated us very well over the last 40 years. We’ve found that if we do everything humanly possible to make our clients happy, then we will continue to stay in business so we can help more people. To be honest, it’s just a win win for both of us.

Now we do more than 5,000 flawless pedigree’s a year! We work well with advertising agencies and farm managers, not to mention printers. We've developed a great deal of expertise in producing sales catalogues and appreciate the pressures involved in running a sale. We strive to be accommodating. Our work is done to SITA standards.
